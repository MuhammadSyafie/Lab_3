About UTHM


Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM), Parit Raja Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM) is one of Malaysia’s public universities, located in the vibrant town of Parit Raja, Batu Pahat, Johor. Known for its focus on engineering, technology, and technical education, UTHM has grown into a respected institution in Malaysia and the ASEAN region. The main campus in Parit Raja is often admired for its modern facilities, serene environment, and strong academic culture. Parit Raja, although a relatively small town, has become a lively hub thanks to the presence of UTHM. The university has played a major role in transforming the area, bringing in students from all over Malaysia and even internationally. This has led to the growth of local businesses such as restaurants, cafes, bookstores, and convenience stores, all of which cater to the daily needs of students. The local community is friendly and welcoming, making it a comfortable and safe place for students to live and study. UTHM itself is well-equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, including large lecture halls, modern laboratories, libraries, sports facilities, and student accommodations. The campus is spacious and green, offering a conducive environment for learning and self-development. Students can participate in a wide range of activities, from academic clubs to sports and cultural societies, allowing them to grow holistically during their time at the university. Academically, UTHM is best known for its strong engineering and technical programs. The university offers undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in various fields such as mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, civil engineering, computer science, and more. It also emphasizes hands-on experience, industrial training, and research projects, ensuring that students are prepared for real-world challenges. Many graduates from UTHM have gone on to successful careers in industry, government, and academia. Another key feature of UTHM is its focus on innovation and research. The university supports various research centers and collaborates with industry partners on projects that contribute to technological advancement and sustainability. This not only benefits the students and staff, but also the broader community and nation. One of the unique aspects of studying at UTHM Parit Raja is the blend of academic life with the local culture. Students often explore nearby places like Batu Pahat town, the local night markets, and recreational spots like Gunung Lambak or Pantai Minyak Beku. Living in Parit Raja allows students to enjoy a more peaceful lifestyle compared to big cities, with lower living costs and less stress. In conclusion, UTHM in Parit Raja offers a well-rounded and fulfilling university experience. It combines quality education, a supportive community, and a pleasant environment — all of which contribute to student success and personal growth. Whether you're interested in engineering, technology, or any of its other programs, UTHM provides the foundation for a promising future.
