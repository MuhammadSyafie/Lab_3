My Hobby: Football

One of my biggest passions is football. I’ve always loved the game — the strategy, the teamwork, and the thrill of scoring a goal.

My top idol in football is Cristiano Ronaldo. His dedication, athleticism, and incredible success story inspired me to take football seriously. Watching him play always gave me the motivation to push harder and never give up.

Because of him, I developed a deep love for the sport. I started playing football actively and eventually joined my high school team. Playing for the school team was one of the most memorable experiences of my life — it taught me discipline, teamwork, and perseverance.

Football is more than just a game for me; it’s something that shaped who I am today.
